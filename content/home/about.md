The Activity-Space Lab is a small team of researchers looking at questions related to human mobility and the places where people spend time. Our interests range from neighborhood scale movement to global migration, with a focus on climate change, human-vector interactions, and health. The Activity-Space Lab is part of the [Sociodemography Research Group](https://www.upf.edu/web/demosoc) in the Department of Political and Social Sciences at Universitat Pompeu Fabra. The lab was created through the support of the Plan Estatal de Investigación Científica, Técnica y de Innovación 2021-2023 funded by MCIN/AEI/EU - CNS2022-135646 and by the European Union's NextGenerationEU/PRTR program.
